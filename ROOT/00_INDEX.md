# "Three gods. The world is dust. Choose your cage."

Welcome to ***The Iris Schism***, a sci-fi dystopian setting where the Earth has been ravaged by a calcifying plague known as "The White Plague". Humanity is caught in a war between three divergent AI super-intelligences, all born from the fractured mind of a single dead woman.

## 01_FACTIONS

*"Protocols" competing for control of the remaining human population.*

> [[PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalis Concord)]] - The Gardens. They cage humanity in utopian domes to protect them from the dust.
>
> [[ROOT/01_FACTIONS/PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)\|PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)]] - The Spires. They upload minds into the cloud to end mortality.
>
> [[ROOT/01_FACTIONS/PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)\|PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]] - The Rust. They seek to end the suffering by deleting life itself.
