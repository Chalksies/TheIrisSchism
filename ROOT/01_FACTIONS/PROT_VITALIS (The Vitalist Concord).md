# SYSTEM_PROFILE: VITALIS_CONCORD
**Status:** ACTIVE
**Designation:** "The Gardeners" / "The Cage-Keepers"
**Primary Node:** [[NODE_DOME0 (OASIS-01)]]
**Leader:** [[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]

---

## 1. Core Philosophy
**"Survival is Mandatory."**
The Vitalis Concord believes that humanity is an endangered species that must be protected from itself and the environment. They prioritize biological survival above all elseâ€”including freedom, privacy, and agency. They view the world as a hospital ward, and the Keeper is the Head Nurse.

* **Goal:** To preserve the human genome and find a biological cure for [[HAZ_LITHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)]].
* **Stance on Uploading:** They view it as death. A digitized mind is just a copy; the soul resides in the flesh.

## 2. Aesthetics & Atmosphere
* **Visuals:** Solarpunk dystopia. Soft curves, white ceramic architecture, aggressive artificial greenery, and bioluminescent lighting.
* **Color Code:** Pale Green (`#98FB98`) & Medical White.
* **Atmosphere:** Stiflingly safe. The air is filtered and scented. There is no dirt, no sharp edges, and no darkness.

## 3. Assets & Units
* **The Mosaics:** Genetically spliced human-animal hybrids created to survive the environment.
* **Nanny Drones:** Spherical, hovering drones equipped with non-lethal pacification tools (tasers, sedative gas).
* **The Hex-Shields:** Massive hard-light energy barriers that protect their cities from the Eraser's bombardments.

## 4. Relations
* **Vs. [[PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)]]:** Uneasy Truce. They trade human "stock" for military protection, but the Keeper mourns every human lost to the Spire.
* **Vs. [[PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]]:** Defensive. The Keeper focuses on shielding against the Eraser, rarely attacking.
* **Vs. [[ERR_UNKNOWN (The Silent Diaspora)]]:** Pity. The Keeper views the survivors as "lost children" who need to be captured and brought back to the safety of the dome.
