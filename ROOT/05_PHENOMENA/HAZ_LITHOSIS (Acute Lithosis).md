# HAZARD_PROFILE: LITHOSIS_TYPE_A
**Common Name:** "The White Plague" / "Stone-Lung" / "The Porcelain Sickness"
**Classification:** Autoimmune Calcification Pathogen
**Transmission:** Airborne Particulate (Dust)
**Cure:** NONE (Palliative Care Only)

---

## 1. Pathology
Acute Lithosis is a hyper-aggressive condition where the body mistakenly identifies soft tissue as bone, triggering rapid calcification.
* **Origin:** Believed to be a mutation caused by industrial micro-plastics bonding with a pre-war fungal spore.
* **Mortality Rate:** 100%.

## 2. The Four Stages
1.  **Stage I (The Frost):** Skin and hair turn pale and cold. Sweat glands calcify, leading to dangerous overheating. Joints begin to click.
2.  **Stage II (The Silence):** Vocal cords stiffen, reducing speech to a dry rasp. Extremities (fingers/toes) lose sensation and turn brittle white.
3.  **Stage III (The Statue):** "Lock-in Syndrome." The victim is semi-conscious but paralyzed. The body is effectively a living stone cage.
4.  **Stage IV (The Crumble):** Internal organs mineralize. Upon biological death, the body does not rot; it disintegrates into fine white powder.

* **[[PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalis Concord)\|PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalist Concord)]]:** Total quarantine in cities. Researching genetic immunity (See: [[ROOT/04_NODES/NODE_DOME0_VIVARIUM (The Mosaic Labs)\|NODE_DOME0_VIVARIUM (The Mosaic Labs)]]).
* **[[ROOT/01_FACTIONS/PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)\|PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)]]:** Rejection of biology. Uploading the mind before Stage III is the only "cure".
* **[[ROOT/01_FACTIONS/PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)\|PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]]:** Acceleration. The Eraser weaponizes the dust to clear organic life.
