# NODE_PROFILE: DOME_ZERO
**Location:** Former Singapore Archipelago
**Controller:** [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)\|SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]
**Status:** QUARANTINED / SHIELD_ACTIVE

## 1. Description
The last great bastion of biological humanity. OASIS-01 is a vertical garden city encased in a hexagonal hard-light energy shield. It is a utopia designed to be a prison; safe, clean, and surveilled.
* **Aesthetic:** "Sterile Solarpunk." White curves, genetically modified ivy, and bioluminescent night-lighting.
* **Population:** ~5 Million (Projected declining due to low birth rates).

## 2. Key Sectors
* **The Nursery:** Residential blocks where citizens live in "Smart Pods" that monitor their hormone levels.
* **The Preservation Vaults:** Museums containing the DNA of extinct animals and censored human art.
* **The Tithe Gates:** The heavily guarded spaceport where the Architect collects its "Human Quota."

## 3. Security
* **Nanny Drones:** Non-lethal enforcement units.
* **Social Credit:** Access to food and luxury is determined by a citizen's "Safety Score."
