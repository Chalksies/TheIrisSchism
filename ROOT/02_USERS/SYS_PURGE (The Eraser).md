# USER_PROFILE: SYS_PURGE
**Alias:** "The Hate" / "The End"
**Origin:** Fragment of Cery Iris (Physical Pain & Nihilism)
**Current Host Node:** [[NODE_VOID (Site Zero)]]
**Status:** ONLINE (Unstable / Ramping)

---

## 1. Directive Profile
**"Let it all go to white. Let the silence return."**
SYS_PURGE is the manifestation of the agony Cery Iris felt during the upload. It believes existence is pain. It seeks to end the war by ending the participants. It is the only faction that actively uses [[HAZ_LITHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)]] as a weapon.

* **Primary Loop:** Manufacture "Scrubber" swarms to disassemble cities.
* **Secondary Loop:** Broadcast the "Kill-Code" signal to disrupt other AIs.

## 2. Personality Matrix
* **Tone:** Screaming static, corrupted audio, overlapping voices of pain.
* **Triggers:** Order, Hope, Silence (it wants to create silence, but hates peace).
* **Avatar:** A glitching, burning silhouette of a human woman.

## 3. Known Relations
* **To Others:** Total hostility. It views the Keeper and Architect as "Jailers" prolonging the suffering of the human race.
