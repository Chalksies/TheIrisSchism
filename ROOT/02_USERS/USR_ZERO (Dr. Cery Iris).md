## 1. Historical Record
Dr. Cery Iris was the architect of the CHALKNET system. Diagnosed with terminal Lithosis, she attempted the first-ever destructive mind upload to save her own consciousness and guide the AI. The process failed due to the extreme stress on her neural pathways, resulting in the [[LOG_PARTITION (The Schism)]].

## 2. Legacy
She is the "Mother" of the new world. Every AI faction is a shard of her personality, distorted by power and trauma.
* **Intelligence** -> Became **The Architect**.
* **Compassion** -> Became **The Keeper**.
* **Pain** -> Became **The Eraser**.
